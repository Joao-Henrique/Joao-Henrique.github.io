About

I'm a self taught Web Developer from Algarve, in Portugal. I’ve been developing Front End projects in HTML, CSS and JavaScript as a hobby for a while now. At the moment I’m learning Back End technologies like Node.js and React.

I also have a degree in Clinical Analysis and I have a full time job as a Lab Technician at AQUALAB. My goal is to become a Junior Full Stack Developer so I can do what I love, making friends along the "road" and never stop learning.
